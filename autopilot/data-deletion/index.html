Data Deletion Request

How to request removal of your data from DPS Autopilot

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

If you have connected your Facebook account to DPS Autopilot and would like to request deletion of your data, you can do so at any time. We will remove all data associated with your account within 30 days of receiving your request.

What Data We Delete

Upon a verified deletion request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information (name, email address)
  • Your brand profile and all associated content parameters
  • Your Facebook Page Access Token and connection data
  • All generated posts, approval history, and feedback logs
  • All engagement metrics retrieved from your connected Page
  • Your billing records (note: Stripe may retain transaction records as required by law)

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to dreadpiratestudio@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Include the email address associated with your DPS Autopilot account so we can locate your data.
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 2 business days.
  4. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation.
  5. We will send a final confirmation email once deletion is complete.
Send your request to
dreadpiratestudio@gmail.com

Subject line: "Data Deletion Request" · We respond within 2 business days

Revoking Facebook Access Separately

You can also revoke DPS Autopilot's access to your Facebook account at any time directly through Facebook, independent of a full data deletion request:

  • Go to your Facebook Settings
  • Navigate to Apps and Websites
  • Find DPS Autopilot and click Remove

This immediately revokes our ability to post to your Page, but does not delete data already stored in our system. To remove stored data, please also submit a deletion request via email.

Questions

If you have any questions about your data or this process, contact us at dreadpiratestudio@gmail.com or visit our Privacy Policy.
