DPS Autopilot — Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective Date: April 26, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Dread Pirate Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates DPS Autopilot, an AI-powered social media content automation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

By signing up for DPS Autopilot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and billing details provided during signup.
  • Brand profile data: Business name, industry, tone preferences, target audience, and example content you provide during onboarding.
  • Facebook Page data: Your Facebook Page ID, Page name, and a Page Access Token obtained through Meta's OAuth process. We use this token solely to publish approved posts on your behalf.
  • Post content and feedback: AI-generated post copy, images, your approval or rejection decisions, and any feedback notes you provide.
  • Engagement metrics: Post performance data (reach, reactions, comments, shares) retrieved from the Meta Graph API to improve content generation over time.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics about how you interact with the DPS Autopilot dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate, schedule, and publish social media content on your connected Facebook Page.
  • To improve AI-generated content quality over time using your approval feedback and post performance data.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To communicate with you about your account, posts, and service updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Facebook Data and Meta Platform Policy

DPS Autopilot accesses your Facebook Page data through Meta's official APIs in compliance with Meta's Platform Terms. Specifically:

  • We request only the permissions necessary to publish posts and read engagement data on your behalf.
  • Your Facebook Page Access Token is stored encrypted and is never shared with third parties.
  • We do not use your Facebook data to serve ads or build advertising profiles.
  • You may revoke our access to your Facebook Page at any time through your Facebook Settings under "Apps and Websites."

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers necessary to operate DPS Autopilot:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Supabase — secure database and file storage
  • Anthropic — AI content generation (post copy only; no personally identifiable information is sent)
  • Ideogram / image generation providers — AI image creation based on prompts derived from your brand profile
  • Bannerbear — branded graphic assembly
  • Cloudflare — hosting and serverless infrastructure

Each provider is bound by their own privacy policy and data processing agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, we will delete your brand profile, Facebook tokens, and post history within 30 days of account closure, unless retention is required by law.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, secure token storage, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Facebook Page access at any time
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dreadpiratestudio@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

DPS Autopilot is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or through the dashboard. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
